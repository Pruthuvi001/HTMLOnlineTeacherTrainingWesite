TTC(Teacher Training Certificate)

Teacher Training Course (TTC) is generally an initial training course for teachers of English as a second/foreign language who have little or no experience in the profession. Its general aim is to develop language awareness and practical skills to provide novice teachers with the knowledge, resources, and above all confidence to stand up in front of EFL learners.
