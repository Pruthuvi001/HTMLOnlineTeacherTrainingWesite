PTC(Primary Training Certificate)

Diploma in Primary Teacher Course, The main aim of PTC program is to cater to the Pre & Primary level teacher workforce in India. It also equips the prospective Pre and Primary teachers with the vital skills and techniques of teaching children aged 2-12 years and make them eligible to teach from Nursery, Montessori and Kindergarten age group till class five.
